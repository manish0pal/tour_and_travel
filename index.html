About Us

People follow different paths to attain knowledge and wisdom. While some prefer worshipping and roving into the scriptures, others prefer travelling to the heart of the culture to experience enlightenment. We help travelers, explorers and discovers find their true selves in the cradle of the holy Ganges River. We take you on a timeless journey to various cities and Buddhist sectors that flourish alongside the banks of the river Ganges.

Travelling brings you closer to the nature around you and also helps you trace the dots of the rich and mysterious Indian civilization. While it can be a memorable and everlasting journey for you, it is surely no less than an adventure. Even if you’re planning to take up this adventure alone, we help you enjoy every bit of it through the widest range of services curated for your needs.

We are the Eternal Ganges. We are the pioneers when it comes to taking you through an exquisite journey of the rich Indian heritage. Through the means of various tours, we help you unearth the Indo Gangetic Planes and rejoice in the taste of the Indian culture. Our exclusive tours take you to the holy city of Varanasi, Prayagraj and more where you can enthral yourself with the sanity, faith, hope, culture and a series of rituals which bring you closer to your self, ultimately closer to the enlightenment.

At Eternal Ganges, we have a team of seasoned experts and professionals who carefully plan holidays for you. We take care of all your travel needs, which is why you can rely on us for comfort, hospitality and quality of services. We are more than just a travel company, we are your gateway to ecstasy, tranquillity and peace. Right from long walks to local cuisine, religious rituals and more, we help you explore the nuances of cities like Varanasi and Prayagraj. You can count on us for taking you through a breathtaking expedition of your lifetime.
